Artist Statement

My work explores the relationship between postmodern discourse and life as performance. With influences as diverse as Kafka and Francis Bacon, new insights are crafted from both constructed and discovered narratives.

Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the endless oscillation of relationships. What starts out as contemplation soon becomes corrupted into a manifesto of distress, leaving only a sense of nihilism and the dawn of a new order.

As temporal forms become transformed through studious and critical practice, the viewer is left with an insight into the darkness of our era.
